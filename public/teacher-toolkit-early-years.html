Reflection & Assessment

Use these quick checks to see what children understood:

  • Can the child name one thing a helper machine could do?
  • Did the child ask a simple question?
  • Did they spot a simple pattern?
Assessment tip: Use a thumbs-up/thumbs-down or smiley faces instead of written tests for this age group.
